Scientifically proven 7 Minute Workout comes to your iPhone and iPad after being a success on Apple TV! Take a break for 7 minutes and be fit. No equipment required! The seven-minute workout, as made popular by a New York Times article covering a circuit workout published in the American College of Sports Medicine's Health & Fitness Journal.
